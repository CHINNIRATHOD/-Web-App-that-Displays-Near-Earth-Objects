CURIOSITIES

Venus has a slow rotation of 243 Earth days, while that its orbit around the Sun lasts 225 Earth days. It is the hottest planet in the solar system with an average temperature on its surface of 460 degrees Celsius.

The atmosphere of Venus is composed mainly of carbon dioxide. carbon and presents a phenomenon known as "superrotation", where the winds at the top of the atmosphere move much faster than the rotation of the planet.

Venus is the brightest planet in the night sky, after the Moon. It is also known for having a dense atmosphere that protects the planet from meteorites, but makes the surface be difficult to study.
