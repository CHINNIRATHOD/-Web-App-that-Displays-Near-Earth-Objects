NEPTUNE

Its name was named in honor of the Roman god of the sea, who comes from Greek mythology. Son of the gods Saturn and Ops, brother of Jupiter and Pluto.

Rule all the waters and seas, and ride the waves on horses whites.

FEATURES

  • AVERAGE TEMPERATURE → -220° C.
  • PRESENCE OF WATER? → Yes, in solid states and gaseous.
  • COMPOSITION OF THE ATMOSPHERE → Hydrogen (74%), helium (24%) and methane (1%).
  • GRAVITY → Slightly higher than that of the Land, approximately 10%
  • SURFACE → 8,083 billion km².
  • LENGTH OF THE DAY → 16H 6' terrestrial.
  • DURATION OF ONE YEAR → 164.8 Earth years.

CURIOSITIES

  • Neptune is the fourth largest planet in the solar system, it has a mass 17 times that of the Earth and its distance from the Sun is 30 times greater.
  • It is the windiest planet in the solar system, with speeds that They can exceed 2,000 km/h.
  • It has a large dark spot, until now it is believed to be a storm system. This spot was discovered by the probe Voyager 2 in 1989.
  • Like Saturn, Neptune also has rings. However, They are much fainter and more difficult to detect than those of Saturn.
