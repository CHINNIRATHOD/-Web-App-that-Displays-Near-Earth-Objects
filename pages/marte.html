MARS

The name "Mars" It is due to the god of war according to Roman mythology, equivalent to the Greek god Ares. The choice of name is due to the fact that the reddish color of the planet resembles the color of blood, making it a symbol of war and violence. Additionally, in astrology, the planet Mars is associated with energy, action and aggressiveness.
