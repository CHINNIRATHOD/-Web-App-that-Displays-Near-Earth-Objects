Why is there life on Earth?

Earth

Its name comes from the Latin "terra" with the same meaning as the ours and related to the verb "torror", which means to dry or singe.

CHARACTERISTICS

TEMPERATURE → The average temperature is 15 °C, thanks to the presence of greenhouse gases in the atmosphere.

PRESENCE OF WATER → The volume of water on earth is of 1500 million Kg³, of which the majority is water salty, although there is also the presence of fresh water in its three states.

ATMOSPHERE → The Earth's atmosphere provides us with gases to breathe, the most important is oxygen. In turn, this protects us from the impact of meteorites.

COMPOSITION OF THE ATMOSPHERE → It is composed of nitrogen, argon, oxygen, dioxide carbon and water vapor.

CURIOSITIES

The day lasts 12 hours and the night lasts 12 hours, an Earth year has a total duration of 365 days. Although every 4 years produces a phenomenon called "leap year" to adjust our calendar to the time it takes the Earth to go around the sun, adding an extra day on February 29, increasing the total length of the year by 366 days. This prevents the seasons from being out of sync with the calendar dates.

Most of the earth's surface is made up of water and the rest is made up of land.

Gravity does not affect all points on Earth in the same way.
